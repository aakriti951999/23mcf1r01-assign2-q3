Ingredients
        100g frozen raspberries
        1 orange, ½ sliced and ½ juiced
        150g porridge oats
        100ml milk
        ½ banana, sliced
        2 tbsp smooth almond butter
        1 tbsp goji berries
        1 tbsp chia seeds
        Method
        STEP 1
        Tip half the raspberries and all of the orange juice in a pan. Simmer until the raspberries soften, about 5 mins.
        
        STEP 2
        Meanwhile stir the oats, milk and 450ml water in a pan over a low heat until creamy.
         Top with the raspberry compote, remaining raspberries, orange slices, banana, almond butter, goji berries and chia seeds.
