why eating well is important ...??

A well-balanced diet provides all of the: energy you need to keep active throughout the day nutrients you need for growth and repair, helping you to stay strong and healthy and help to prevent diet-related illness, such as some cancers Nutrient Supply: Consuming a balanced diet ensures that your body receives essential nutrients, including vitamins, minerals, proteins, carbohydrates, and fats, necessary for optimal functioning. Energy Boost: A well-balanced diet provides the energy your body needs for daily activities. Carbohydrates, in particular, are a primary source of energy. Weight Management: Eating a healthy diet can help in maintaining a healthy weight. It can prevent both obesity and undernutrition, contributing to overall well-being. Disease Prevention: A nutritious diet is associated with a lower risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and certain cancers. It also supports a strong immune system, helping the body fight off illnesses.
